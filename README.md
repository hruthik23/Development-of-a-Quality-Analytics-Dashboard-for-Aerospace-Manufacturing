# Development-of-a-Quality-Analytics-Dashboard-for-Aerospace-Manufacturing
Lean Six Sigma Optimisation App for Quality Department 
The aerospace and defence manufacturing industry has some of the most stringent regulatory and operational guidelines in the world. Aerospace components, being critical components, have extremely high manufacturing tolerances, and there is no room for error whatsoever. The aerospace and defence manufacturing industry has traditionally employed traditional quality management systems that involve rigorous inspection, statistical process control, and corrective action plans.
With the advent of Industry 4.0, the data generated from manufacturing operations, in terms of quantity, velocity, and variety, has outgrown the capabilities of traditional quality control methodologies, which employ spreadsheets and traditional data analysis techniques. The manufacturing industry has now begun adopting "Quality 4.0," which involves the use of digitalization, advanced data analytics, and machine learning technologies in traditional quality management methodologies (Radziwill, 2018). Despite this paradigm shift in quality management methodologies, many mid-to-large tier aerospace manufacturing suppliers face difficulties in effectively managing data silos, delayed reporting, and inability to identify root causes of defects before incurring financial loss.
